Plato's Pizza 2015: A Maven Pizza Challenge

A Comprehensive Sales Dashboard Using Microsoft Excel 2016

INTRODUCTION
The data presented in this dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of a specific business's operational performance during the calendar year of 2015. With the duration spanning from January 1 to December 31, encompassing 358 days, the dashboard offers a valuable insight into various facets of the business. From the busiest day and time of operation to the quantity of pizza produced during peak hours, the best and worst-selling pizza and the average order quantities, these statistics offer detailed snapshot of the business's activities and customer preferences.

DASHBOARD
Target Audience : Mario Maven (Manager, Plato's Pizza)


KEY INSIGHTS

Busiest Day and Time : Fridays at 12 noon emerged as the peak operating hours, witnessing the highest customer influx.

Total Pizza Made During Peak Hours : An impressive 6,776 pizzas were crafted during the busiest times, highlighting the efficiency of operations of operations during these hours.

Best Selling and Worst-Selling Pizza : The Thai Chicken Pizza stood out as the best-selling item on the menu, demonstrating its popularity among customers, on the other hand, The Brie Carrie Pizza struggled to gain traction and ranked as the least popular choice among patrons.

Average Quantity Ordered : On average, customers ordered 3, 305 pizzas, indicating the overall demand and consumption trends during the year.
