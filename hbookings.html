Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA), Predicting Hotel Cancellations

Background

   This Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) supports a hotel with a project aimed to increase revenue from their room bookings. The company believe that they can use data science to help them reduce the number of cancellations. This EDA used appropriate methodology to identify what contributes to a whether a booking will be fulfilled or cancelled. The company intend to use the results of this EDA to reduce the chance someone cancels their booking.

Business Task

   1. What factors affect whether customers cancel their booking?
   2. Are cancellations more likely during weekends?
   3. Which general recommendations for the hotel can you make?

Executive Summary

   Based on the data presented. The total number of bookings are 36,275 with 33% cancelled and 77% not cancelled. Year 2018 had the highest number of cancellations than year 2017. Most of the reservations are happened during Weekends. The factors that affect the customer to cancel their bookings are change of plans, scheduling conflicts, etc. It might be possible that the reservation is a free of charge which is beneficial to the hotel guest. It is easy to cancel a reservation if it is free upon cancelling the reservations.

Executive Summary

   To minimize the number of cancellations, hotels can take certain steps such as imposing non- refundable rates, taking deposits, and enforcing stricter policies for cancellations. Offering early bird rates with different time frames can also be an effective way to encourage guests to make reservations in advance and keep them. Hotels can also consider providing special discounts for direct booking as it can help build stronger relationships with guests.

   In Addition, hotels should focus on fulfilling special request made by the guests to enhance their satisfaction and reduce likelihood of cancellations. It's also important to monitor the reasons behind cancellations, such as market segments and distribution of channels, to identify areas of improvement and develop targeted solutions.

   By leveraging data-driven insights and implementing strategic measures, hotels can significantly reduce cancellations, enhance customer satisfaction, and ultimately boost their profitability.
