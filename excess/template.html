Conclusions

  Based on data presented, the most profitable Month was December with the total profit of 2.7 Million and the most profitable year was 2014 with the total profit of 16.8 Million.

   The most successful country when it comes to profit was France with the total of 3.78 Million followed by Germany with the total of 3.68 Million.

   Since the most profitable product is Paseo with the total profit of 4.8 Million and the most profitable Segment is Government with the total profit of 11.39 Million, the company should continue to invest in Paseo as a product and Government as a segment.

Recommendations

They could conduct market research to understand the consumer behavior, preferences, and trends in these countries to develop targeted marketing strategies.

   Increase Production of Paseo: Since it is the most profitable product, the company could consider increasing its prouction to meet the demand for this product.    Optimize Marketing and Sales Efforts During December: The company could leverage social media, email marketing and other channels to promote their products and services and offer incentives to customers to drive sales.
